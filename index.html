About Me

I am Wenjia Li, a tenured Associate Professor in the College of Engineering and Computing Sciences at New York Institute of Technology (NYIT). I received my Ph.D degree in Computer Science from UMBC in 2011, under the supervision of Professor Anupam Joshi. Before that, I got my B.Eng. and M.Eng. degrees from Hunan University in China. My research interests are broadly in the area of cyber security and trust, networking, mobile computing, and machine learning.

I'm always looking for self-motivated students at all levels (both undergraduates and graduates including PhD and Master's students) who are interested in pursuing research in security and trust for wireless networks, connected vehicles, IoT systems, and mobile (Android) devices. Please stop by my office or send me an email with a brief introduction and CV of yourself. Thank you!
